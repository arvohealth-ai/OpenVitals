# Commercial Licensing

OpenVitals is source-available for noncommercial use under the PolyForm Noncommercial License 1.0.0.

Commercial use requires a separate written commercial license from the project maintainers. Examples of commercial use include selling hosted OpenVitals services, bundling OpenVitals into a paid product, using OpenVitals to provide paid health/wellness services, or operating it as part of a commercial internal workflow.

For a commercial license, open a private contact channel with the maintainers and include:

- intended product or deployment model
- expected number of users or seats
- whether real provider data, mobile collectors, or agent automation will be used
- support, security, and compliance requirements

Do not use OpenVitals commercially until a commercial license is signed.
